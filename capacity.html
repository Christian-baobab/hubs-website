Capacity Building

The Hubs Capacity Building program focuses on building local capacity for continuously improved program delivery, monitoring and reiteration. The Hubs are available to support partners by delivering training modules.

What We Do

Our Capacity BuildingProgramming includes:

1. Facilitation Certificate Program: offering up to 100 hours of training, this program fully equips facilitators with pedagogical tools to support - and even lead - student-centered classes, including the use of rubrics, lesson plans, and how to tutor without editing. Facilitators also learn how to identify and refer psychosocial cases, to perform administrative tasks, and to manage a classroom.
2. Mentorship Training: offering up to 100 hours of instruction, this program trains teachers and administrators in pedagogical tools for training their teachers as well as for leading meetings, educational leadership, and tools for holding one-on-one and group mentorship sessions.
3. Administrative capacity-building for refugee-led organizations (RLO's) hosting Hubsâ€™ programs: since 2020, we have prioritized implementing programming in collaboration with refugee-led organizations.
4. Researcher training for refugees interested in tracking and researching Hubs-related work.
