About Us

What We Do

The Hubs deliver learner-centered, contextualized programming that is innovative in its use of technology to support participants in low-resource settings and builds local capacity to support implementation. The Hubs currently operate in Eastern Africa, the MENA region, and South Asia.

Our Programs
Higher Education Access

The Hubs offer displaced youth the opportunity to upskill their critical thinking, academic writing, and analysis skills in preparation for admission to university, a diploma track or a certificate program.

Capacity Building

The Hubs implement Capacity Building at sites where such informal or parallel education systems are in operation.

Research

We promote Refugee and Host Community-Led research as critical to teaching and learning, supporting collaboration across borders, barriers and boundaries.

What Our Students Say

Maisam Alhaj Mohammad
Hubs Student, Jordan

This allows me to exchange ideas, improve my language skills, and build connections with other students.”

Alamin Jebrin Tutu
Hubs Alumnus, Kenya
