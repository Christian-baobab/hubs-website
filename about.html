About Us

Our Vision

The Hubs for Connected Learning Initiatives (Hubs) envision a world in which all persons affected by crisis and/or displacement have equal access to post-secondary opportunities to achieve self-reliance. The Hubs provide innovative, learner-centered, technologically connected programming situated at refugee-led organizations in refugee camps, while also advocating for educational access globally.
